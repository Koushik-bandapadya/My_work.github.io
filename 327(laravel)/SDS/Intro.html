1. Introduction

The world is dependent to internet in this era. From news to job circular everything is searched over the web. Searching for a home for rent physically is very time consuming as well as tiring therefore a good website for property search is all we need in our busy life. The purpose is to make an app to rent property and for the sake of easier life. Property rental service allows checking hundreds of houses over a span of some minutes. Searching categorically according to desired location, rental cost, and space is offered. An option to see trending deals can be seen as well. The customer can have a map view and see the available property deals in the particular area and can also access more detail of any particular available property.
